The "Erasmus experience"

More than 9 million people have participated in the Erasmus programme since its creation. The number of young participants has increased significantly since 1987. Nearly 300,000 a year compared with only 3,244 in 1987. Spain is the country that has allowed most people to participate in Erasmus with more than 40,000 per year, slightly ahead of France, Germany and Italy.

Thomas More offers exchange programmes in English, for students from partner universities. We welcome exchange students from our partner universities around the globe to study or do an internship. Joining one of our programmes for a semester or longer will give your degree an international boost.
